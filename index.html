Escape the Titanic

Escape the Titanic is a VR Maze game created with Unity for the Oculus headset. You are a crewmate on the Titanic who woke up in their room to a sinking ship. You are stuck below deck and must find your way out. The exits may or may not be accessible due to water, damage, and darkness. Your task is to find the missing oar and return to the escape boat on the upper level within 5-minutes.

AI Tile Placement

You are given a landscape on which certain "bushes" grow, marked by colors: 1, 2, 3, 4. The landscape is of square shape, so, it might be 100 x 100 or 200 x 200 etc. You are given a set of “tiles” which are of three different shapes. The tiles are 4 x 4. One tile only covers part of the landscape. You are given a "taget" of which bushes should be visible after you have finished placing the tiles. Write a CSP algorithm to solve this problem.
